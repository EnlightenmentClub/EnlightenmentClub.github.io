    "Why should I join Enlightenment Club? "
    This surely is the question which needs to be answered. Every club has it's own aura and principles which stay constant throughout. Enlightenment has it's own as well. Here, people believe in nurturing personalities. It's a place where you can evolve and explore your interests as an individual. Enlightenment evolves right along with you. It aligns with your interests as they change. Communication, Aptitude and Coding. We emphasize on the importance of these three aspects as they would help you in each endeavour you wish to take in your college and there after. Each of these will only add to your strengths. It doesn't matter if you are a novice or an expert in any of them. Enlightenment has a special place for everyone. We merely don't teach or learn here. We have wonderful discussions. We make memories. We evolve. Come, join us if you wish to do the same!!
